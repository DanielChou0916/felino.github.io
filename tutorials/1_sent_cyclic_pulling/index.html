Single Edge-Notch Tension (SENT) Fatigue Analysis

A \(1 \text{mm}\times 1\text{mm}\) Single Edge-Notch Tension fatigue test is conducted in the presented page. The goals are

  • Show capability of Felino to capture degradation fracture patterns of fatigue behavior.
  • Compare results from different fatigue energy counting.
