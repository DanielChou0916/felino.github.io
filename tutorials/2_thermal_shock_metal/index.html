Fatigue Behavior Under Thermal Shock

A 1 mm \(\times\) 1 mm Single Edge-Notch Tension fatigue test is set up on this page (again), this time, the effect of thermal shock is considered. Instead of cyclic displacement, the cyclic stress is applied on the top-left corner. And the top is heating up after approximaterly 15 loading cycles. Objectives are:

  • Provide the standard format for thermal fatigue simulation for users.
  • Compare results under different temperature increment \(\Delta T\).
