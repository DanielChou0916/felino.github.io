3D Simulations for Composite Material

This test uses a concrete cylinder 2D model which is a matrix-inclusion structure to demonstrate a case of 3D simulations.

  • To provide an example set of three dimensional simulations.
  • To double check that combination of two materials should lead to numerical results that bonded within the strength range of two materials.

Material Parameters and Boundary Conditions

Material parameters are listed in the format (aggregate, cement). For example, Young's modulus is given as \(E = (E_\text{agg}; E_\text{ce})\).
